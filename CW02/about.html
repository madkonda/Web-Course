My story

Hello there! I’m Madhu, a master’s student in Computer Science at Georgia State University. I love solving algorithmic challenges, building systems that make life a little easier, and hacking on side projects. I like projects that turn messy problems into clear workflows.

I’ve worked across both frontend and backend web technologies, mostly in React, Python, Java, and TypeScript. I also enjoy the terminal and have built command-line tools—there’s something satisfying about fast, keyboard-first workflows.

Outside tech, my favorite hobby is playing cricket and watching cricket. Cricket taught me to think a few moves ahead, stay calm under pressure, and keep improving even when progress feels slow.

This page is built to practice semantic HTML and content structure. It’s not meant to be my main personal website; it’s a hands-on exercise to strengthen fundamentals.
